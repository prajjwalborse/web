About

At UpwardLending.ca, we are dedicated to enriching lives through sustainable financial growth. With a focus on real estate-backed opportunities, we go beyond transactions to build meaningful relationships that offer financial security for our investors and the stability of real estate for our borrowers.

Backed by decades of collective experience in the mortgage and private lending industry, we pride ourselves on delivering excellence, leadership, and trust.

Our mission is to connect borrowers with the funds they need to leverage their real estate to assist them either through a difficult time or to optimize their investment opportunity.

As the real estate lending landscape continues to shift, Upward Lending remains at the forefront of Canada’s private lending industry, driven by core values of trust, transparency, and opportunity. We believe every borrower deserves a fair and flexible borrowing experience, and every investor deserves access to high-quality, real estate-backed investments.

  • Personalized Mortgage Investments: Designed to match each investor’s unique goals and risk tolerance.
  • Rigorous Due Diligence: Ensuring every opportunity meets our high standards of quality and security.
  • Expertise and Innovation: Backed by years of experience in Vancouver’s dynamic real estate market.
